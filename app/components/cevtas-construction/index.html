Construction & Commisioning

We operate in an interdisciplinary framework of environmental and renewable energy technologies, sustainability and investment. Mainly, we design, build and operate waste water treatment plants and other critical water infrastructure projects under the environmental technologies branch along with large-scale commercial and industrial solar and wind farms, biomass and geothermal power plants under the renewable energy branch. We offer comprehensive project management that includes deployment, installation, commissioning and handover, followed by fine-tuning, maintenance and support.

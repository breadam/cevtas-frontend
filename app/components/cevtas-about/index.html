Company Overview

Urban and industrial development and modernization all over the world has presented themselves with environmental pollution and energy problems. These must be the main target of the research and development studies today for the sustainability of our existence. When it comes to develop environmental technologies, we apply the highest technology and pay special attention to energy balance and the economics of our projects. Since our establishment in 1985, our understanding and principles have made us a pioneer and reliable company in our sectors. Today, we continue our work in both national and international areas and sustain our understanding of high standard, modern technology, service, accountability and quality. We provide services for municipalities, industrial organized zones and related industrial establishments along with residential and commercial structures on a turn-key basis. We are determined to continue on our activities in the fields of environmental technologies, alternative and renewable energy systems and sustainable community development. We have been carrying on consultancy, business development, process design, manufacturing of innovative process equipment along with construction and operation of such projects on a turn-key basis.

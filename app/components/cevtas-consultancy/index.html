Consultancy & Business Development

Our Consultancy and Business Development department provides strategic consultancy focused on sustainable water and energy investments, helping our investors to understand and seize local and global opportunities, overcome regulative, technological, financial and strategic challenges within their prospective markets. Applying a bottom-up approach in market and competition analysis, strategy development, business planning and due diligence processes, our team support its qualitative findings with state-of-the-art techno-economic modeling and financial analysis tools to aid our investors in their decision making processes.

Our team has assessed markets and technologies, along with individual project performance and market position in order to optimally diversify the investments of our clientele.

Our work include simultaneous audits of solar, wind, biomass and geothermal energy investments along with assessments of upcoming issues, relevant policies and regulations and their impact on the wider markets and their impact on project level investments which fuels us with the required knowledge and decision making capabilities to advise our clients drawing on our detailed insights into technical, financial and environmental performance of future projects and maximize their returns.
